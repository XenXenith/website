Payment Process

To ensure trust and satisfaction, I follow a transparent payment process:

  • I will edit 10 to 40 seconds of the video (depending on the video length) and send it to you in 360p resolution.
  • You pay 10% of the total cost after viewing the initial edit.
  • I then complete half of the video and send it to you in 360p resolution.
  • You pay 50% of the total cost after viewing the half-completed video.
  • Finally, I complete the video and send it to you in any resolution of your choice or a ProRes file if you like.
